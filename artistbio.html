DORTE JUUL

Dorte Juul

FOLLOW

SHARE

Dorte Juul goes to Guldborgsund Art School in the 5th year now, and actively participates in courses/exhibitions. She is 56 years old, active nurse, lives in South Zealand and besides painting, she loves being with her family and enjoying their summer house. Dorte loves new challenges, and therefore likes to paint to order, with her own style.

“My art is original, I love to unfold my creative thoughts and get them on canvas. I get inspired by many everyday experiences and stay in the present when I paint. I love using colors, painting original works as well as abstract ones. I like to paint orders, though with my own artistic touch on the painting.”
